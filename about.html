Our Mission and Team

Our Mission

VigiQR was created with a simple yet important mission: to protect users from the growing threat of QR code phishing attacks.

As QR codes become increasingly ubiquitous in our daily lives—from restaurant menus to payment systems and marketing campaigns—cybercriminals have seized the opportunity to exploit them for malicious purposes. Our tool provides a safe way to verify QR codes before scanning them with your primary devices.

We believe that cybersecurity should be accessible to everyone, not just technical experts. That's why we've designed VigiQR to be intuitive, user-friendly, and educational, helping users build better security habits while providing immediate protection.
